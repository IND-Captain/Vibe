The Problem

Critical global challenges we are solving

Billions of people face preventable suffering every day. We identified 6 interconnected global crises — and built technology to address them.

🏚️
No Poverty
800 Million people affected

Over 800 million people still live in extreme poverty, struggling daily to meet basic needs like food, health care, and clean water. Poverty traps communities in cycles of hardship across generations.

SDG Goal 1
🌾
Zero Hunger
735 Million chronically hungry

Chronic hunger affects approximately 735 million people globally, driven by food system inefficiencies, conflict, and climate disruptions. Wasteful supply chains leave billions without nutritional security.

SDG Goal 2
❤️
Good Health & Well-being
Preventable deaths daily

High maternal mortality rates and lack of universal health coverage continue to cause preventable deaths worldwide, especially in low-income and conflict-affected regions.

SDG Goal 3
📚
Quality Education
Millions lack access

Millions of children lack access to equitable education, often due to high dropout rates, language barriers, infrastructure gaps, and economic pressure on families.

SDG Goal 4
⚡
Affordable & Clean Energy
Billions without modern energy

Billions of people still lack access to modern, sustainable energy, relying instead on polluting fuels that harm health and limit economic opportunity.

SDG Goal 7
💼
Decent Work & Economic Growth
Persistent informal employment

Slow global GDP growth and persistent informal employment hinder the creation of quality jobs. Millions lack fair wages, job security, and access to economic opportunity.

How It Works

From data to real-world impact

Our platform collects, analyzes, and acts on data from communities to drive measurable improvements across all four domains.

01
Identify Communities in Need

We use geospatial data, census statistics, and community surveys to pinpoint regions with the highest need across education, food, health, and employment.

02
Deploy AI-powered Tools

Our domain-specific AI tools are deployed directly to users via mobile-first web applications — accessible even on low-bandwidth connections.

03
Measure & Amplify Impact

We continuously track outcomes across all four domains and surface insights to NGO partners, governments, and communities to amplify what works.
